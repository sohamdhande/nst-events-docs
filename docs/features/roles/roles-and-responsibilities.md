# Roles and Responsibilities

## Hierarchy
The NST-Events platform enforces the following role hierarchy (from highest to lowest privilege):

1. **Platform Admin**
2. **Faculty Admin**
3. **Faculty Mentor**
4. **Club Admin**
5. **Core Member**
6. **Member**
7. **Student**

## Detailed Responsibilities

### 1. Platform Admin
* **Description**: Superuser of the entire platform. Typically IT staff or core platform developers.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Manage global platform settings.
  * Oversee all clubs, events, and users.
  * Resolve critical system-wide issues and audit logs.

### 2. Faculty Admin
* **Description**: University administration staff overseeing all campus activities.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * View high-level analytics and reports for all clubs.
  * Step in to approve or reject events if Faculty Mentors are unavailable.
  * Enforce campus-wide rules and guidelines.

### 3. Faculty Mentor
* **Description**: Faculty assigned to guide and oversee specific clubs.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Review and approve/reject events created by their assigned clubs.
  * View attendance and engagement analytics for their clubs.
  * Provide governance and oversight.
  * *Note*: Cannot mark attendance for themselves or generate check-in QR codes.

### 4. Club Admin
* **Description**: Student leader (e.g., President, Vice President) of a club.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Create and manage events for their club.
  * Assign Core Members and Members.
  * Generate dynamic QR codes for event check-ins.
  * View event-specific analytics.

### 5. Core Member
* **Description**: Trusted student committee member of a club.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Assist in organizing events.
  * Can view internal club data and help manage event logistics.
  * Generate QR codes if delegated by Club Admin.

### 6. Member
* **Description**: Official member of a club.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Participate in club-restricted events.
  * Access club-specific announcements.

### 7. Student
* **Description**: Base authenticated user in the platform.
* **Responsibilities**:
  * Browse public events.
  * Register for events and teams.
  * Scan dynamic QR codes to mark attendance.
  * Track personal points on the individual leaderboard.
