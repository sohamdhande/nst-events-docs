# Operations Mode

## Purpose
A specialized, distraction-free interface for **Live Event Execution**.

## Activation & Exit Conditions
* **Activation**: Enabled by a Club Admin or Core Member up to 2 hours before an event starts.
* **Exit**: Explicit "End Operations Mode" toggle, or automatically 2 hours after event completion.

## Capabilities & QR Operations
* Casts the secure, rotating Dynamic QR TOTP to a projector/secondary screen.
* Disables standard sidebar navigation to prevent accidental clicks.

## Attendance Monitoring & Manual Verification
Provides a live feed of students checking in. 
Allows Core Members to manually verify attendance for students with broken phones (bypassing QR, tracked securely in audit logs).

## Emergency Procedures
Includes a "Lockdown Event" button to instantly halt all check-ins and registrations.
