# Future Expansion Strategy

## Out of Scope Features (V1)
The following modules have been officially documented as out of scope for the V1 release, to be revisited in later iterations:
* **Advanced Visual Effects**: Custom GPU shaders, Liquid Glass rendering, and advanced graphics programming.
* **Future Campus Modules**: e.g., integrated cafeteria ordering or library seat booking.
* **Future Recruitment Features**: Alumni networking or job boards.
* **Future Certificates**: On-chain verifiable digital signatures for event certificates.
* **Future Analytics**: Complex predictive attendance modeling.
