# ADR-030: Attendance fraud mitigation is a first-class requirement

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Historical data shows high rates of proxy attendance at campus events.

## Decision
Attendance fraud mitigation is a first-class requirement.

## Consequences
Positive: Campus attendance abuse is expected; addressing it proactively protects platform credibility.
Negative: Increases technical complexity heavily.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Trust-based system**: Rejected.

## Future Impact
Will continuously shape feature development.
