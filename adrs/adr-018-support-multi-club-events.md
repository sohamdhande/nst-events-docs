# ADR-018: Support multi-club events

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Campus events are frequently collaborative efforts.

## Decision
Support multi-club events.

## Consequences
Positive: Joint club events are common. This allows shared administration and visibility.
Negative: Many-to-many relationship increases query complexity.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Single club owner**: Rejected as it prevents collaboration.

## Future Impact
Enables accurate cross-club analytics.
