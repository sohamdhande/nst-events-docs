# ADR-011: Support multi-role users

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Real NST operations require overlapping responsibilities (e.g., Student + Club Admin).

## Decision
Support multi-role users.

## Consequences
Positive: Accurately reflects campus reality.
Negative: Increases UI and permission check complexity.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Strict single role**: Rejected as it limits student involvement.

## Future Impact
Requires a flexible RBAC schema.
