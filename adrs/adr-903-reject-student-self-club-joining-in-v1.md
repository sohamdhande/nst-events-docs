# ADR-903: Reject Student Self Club Joining in V1

## Status
Rejected

## Context
Students want a button to immediately join any club.

## Decision
Reject Student Self Club Joining in V1.

## Consequences
Positive: Club recruitment workflows are complex and belong in future scope.
Negative: More manual work for V1 launch.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Self-serve joining**: Deferred.

## Future Impact
Ensures V1 can be delivered in 2 months.
