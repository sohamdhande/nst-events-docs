# ADR-009: Use Google OAuth

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Users need a frictionless way to sign up and log in without remembering passwords.

## Decision
Use Google OAuth.

## Consequences
Positive: Institutional authentication requirement is easily met.
Negative: Dependent on Google's uptime.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Email/Password**: Rejected due to security risks and poor UX.

## Future Impact
Simplifies identity management.
