# ADR-028: Introduce faculty roles

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Campus events require official oversight.

## Decision
Introduce specific faculty roles (Faculty Mentor, Faculty Admin).
**Faculty Permissions defined:**
* **Can:** Create events, Approve events, View attendance, View analytics, View reports.
* **Cannot:** Mark attendance (as a student), Generate check-in QR codes, Modify attendance records manually, Change platform settings.

## Consequences
Positive: Faculty oversight and governance ensures compliance with university policies. Clear separation of duties.
Negative: Adds another persona to the system.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Student-only platform**: Rejected.

## Future Impact
Paves the way for automated approval workflows and institutional reporting.
