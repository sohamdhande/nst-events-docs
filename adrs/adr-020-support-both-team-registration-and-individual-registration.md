# ADR-020: Support both team registration and individual registration

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Hackathons and competitions require team structures.

## Decision
Support both team registration and individual registration.

## Consequences
Positive: Hackathons and competitions require team support natively.
Negative: Complicates the registration schema.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Individual only**: Rejected as organizers would have to use external tools.

## Future Impact
Makes the platform a one-stop-shop for all event types.
