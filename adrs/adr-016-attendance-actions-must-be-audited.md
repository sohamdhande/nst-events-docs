# ADR-016: Attendance actions must be audited

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Attendance data affects student records and potential grading.

## Decision
Attendance actions must be audited.

## Consequences
Positive: Essential for dispute resolution and fraud investigations.
Negative: Increased storage requirements.

## Alternatives Considered
* **No audit trails**: Rejected due to academic compliance risks.

## Future Impact
Ensures trust in the platform's data.
