# ADR-901: Reject Static QR Codes

## Status
Rejected

## Context
Static QR codes are easy to generate and print.

## Decision
Reject the use of static QR codes.

## Consequences
Positive: Forces the system to be more secure.
Negative: Easy to abuse by sharing photos of the QR.

## Alternatives Considered
* **Dynamic QR**: Accepted instead.

## Future Impact
Establishes a baseline of security.
